🌍 About Travox

Travox is a smart tourism companion platform designed to simplify travel, enhance safety, and provide personalized experiences for tourists.

🎯 What is Travox?

Travox helps travelers plan their journey efficiently by integrating multiple services into one platform — including hotels, transport, food recommendations, and local guides.

⚙️ Key Features

  • ✔ Budget-based hotel recommendations
  • ✔ Food suggestions based on location & preference
  • ✔ Transport price estimator (Uber-like)
  • ✔ Ticket booking system (bus, train, flight)
  • ✔ Local guide connection with safety features
  • ✔ Emergency SOS support

📱 How Travox Works

  • 1️⃣ Choose your destination
  • 2️⃣ Plan budget (hotel + food + transport)
  • 3️⃣ Book tickets and services
  • 4️⃣ Connect with local guides
  • 5️⃣ Travel safely with real-time support

💡 Use Cases

  • ✔ Solo travelers looking for safety
  • ✔ Tourists exploring new cities
  • ✔ Budget travel planning
  • ✔ Finding trusted local guides

🚀 Why Travox?

Unlike traditional travel apps, Travox combines multiple services into one platform — making it easier, faster, and safer for travelers.
